Welcome

In Ethiopia, it is commonly referred to as an “Ekub”, which in English is a Rotating Savings
and Credit Association (ROSCA). This is where a pot of funds is collected by a group of people
who each contribute the same amount and then one person every meeting is selected at random
to receive the pot.
